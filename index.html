CULINARY GATEWAY

The Spirit of Hebir Bole

Located on the bustling Africa Avenue, Hebir Ethiopia Cultural Restaurant stands as a vibrant gateway to authentic Ethiopian cuisine fused with diverse African and Middle Eastern influences.

We embrace the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia, offering guests a genuine taste experience marked by classic dishes and modern international favorites. Our atmosphere is cozy, welcoming, and relaxed—a Favored spot for locals and global visitors alike.

TRADITION MEETS VARIETY

Ethiopian Culinary Heritage

The foundation of our kitchen is the careful preparation of **Injera** and the slow-simmered **Berbere** spice blends. Our stews burst with deep, authentic flavors, showcasing the care invested in each preparation.

Yet, we also pay tribute to international palates. Whether it's the Fish Goulash or our robust Carnivore Pizza, our menu ensures that tradition and variety live side-by-side in perfect harmony.

VIBRANT ATMOSPHERE

Cultural Dance & Football

Hebir is more than a restaurant; it’s a cultural hub. On special nights, experience the electric energy of traditional **Eskista** dance and live Ethiopian music.

During match days, the energy shifts to the screens. Watch your favorite football teams with a cold draft beer in hand—choose your size, from standard to our famous 1-litre **"Jambo"** glasses.

Eskista Dance Nights

Traditional shoulder dance performances every Friday & Saturday.

Match Day Live

Premier League & Champions League on wide screens with Jambo Beer specials.

Buna & Beer

From the ritualistic aroma of the Ethiopian Coffee Ceremony to the refreshing chill of a draft beer. Hebir caters to every mood, making it suitable for a casual lunch or a shared dinner with community.
